Mattar Panner

45 minutes - Veg Dish

Ingredients

1 cup cubed paneer ( Indian Cottage cheese) 1 medium onion,
skinless 1 clove garlic 1 teaspoon grated ginger 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1 teaspoon cumin powder 2 teaspoons Tomato Paste,
mixed thoroughly in a cup of water 2 teaspoons kasuri methi ( dried fenugreek leaves) 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream salt to taste 1/2
teaspoon sugar 1 teaspoon canola oil 2 teaspoons Kashmiri mirch ( Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder)


Steps

### Recipe for Mattar Paneer

            #### **Ingredients**
            - **For the gravy:**
              - 1 medium onion, finely chopped
              - 2 tomatoes, pureed
              - 1 teaspoon grated ginger
              - 1 clove garlic, minced
              - 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
              - 1 teaspoon red chili powder
              - 1 teaspoon coriander powder
              - 1 teaspoon cumin powder
              - 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
              - 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
              - 2 tablespoons cooking oil or ghee
              - 1/2 cup heavy cream or fresh cream
              - Salt to taste
              - 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional)
            
            - **For the dish:**
              - 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
              - 200g paneer, cubed
              - 1-2 cups water (adjust based on desired consistency)
              - Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
            
            ---
            
            #### **Steps**
            
            1. **Prepare the Base:**
               - Heat 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee in a pan.
               - Add the chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
               - Add the ginger and garlic, and cook for another minute until fragrant.
            
            2. **Add Spices and Tomatoes:**
               - Stir in the turmeric, red chili powder, cumin powder, and coriander powder.
               - Cook for 30 seconds to release the aroma of the spices.
               - Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts separating from the mixture.
            
            3. **Cook the Peas:**
               - Add the green peas to the gravy and mix well.
               - Pour in 1 to 1.5 cups of water, depending on the consistency you prefer.
               - Simmer for 5-7 minutes until the peas are tender.
            
            4. **Add Paneer:**
               - Add the paneer cubes to the gravy and gently stir to coat them.
               - Simmer for another 2-3 minutes to let the paneer absorb the flavors.
            
            5. **Finish the Dish:**
               - Stir in the cream to make the gravy rich and creamy.
               - Add garam masala, kasuri methi (crushed between your palms), and sugar (optional).
               - Simmer for 1-2 minutes and adjust salt as needed.
            
            6. **Garnish and Serve:**
               - Turn off the heat and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
               - Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
